This is the moves pages. If you hover over the move, it will display the hitboxes. Green = Hurtbox, Red = Hitbox.


Standing Kick

This move very fast at only 6 frames, and can lead to good combos on hit, or when blocked, leaves the player at an advantage, so you can continue pressure.

Forward Punch

A slow move that boast a large amount of upper body invinciblity, allowing it to blow through the opponent's attacks. If it interrupts the opponent, which causes a counterhit, it can lead to devastating combos.

Crouching Heavy Slash

Slayer stomps the ground, causing a shockwave which will stagger the opponent, which can be followed up with a basic combo and knockdown. During the startup, he hops, so it can evade some low-hitting moves.

Pilebunker

A huge punch which does a lot of damage, but is unsafe on block. It is a prime combo starter on counter hit, and can be powered up for more damage.

Big Bang Upper

A very fast move that has an amazing hitbox. It is very good at hitting opponents in the air, and when it hits launches the opponent all the way off-screen, leading to easy combos.
