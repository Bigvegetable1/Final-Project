This is the moves pages. If you hover over the move, it will display the hitboxes. Green = Hurtbox, Red = Hitbox.


Standing Kick

This move is fast, can hit the opponent for a long time, and can lead to knockdowns. It is a important move to use.

Crouching Slash

This is a strong move with a great hitbox. Its speed is decent for its reach. It also brings the opponent closer to you, allowing pressure continuation, or suprise the opponent with a grab.

Standing Slash

A far reaching move that has many uses. It is good at hitting opponents on the ground, as well as in the air.

Crouching Heavy Slash

A slow, strong move that is great at hitting the opponent directly above you. It can lead to very high damage combos.

Potemikin buster

The best grab in the game. It boasts incredible reach, astonishing damage, and knocks down leading to more pressure.
